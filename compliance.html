Our Compliance Commitment

Transparency and legality form the foundation of investor confidence.

At Crowsfiddled Company Limited, we operate in full adherence to Moroccan and international real estate investment frameworks. Our compliance ensures that every property, investor fund, and transaction is lawfully registered, verifiable, and protected under official regulatory supervision.

Regulatory Oversight & Legal Framework

ANCFCC — Land & Title Authority

Every property managed or facilitated by Crowsfiddled is officially registered under the Agence Nationale de la Conservation Foncière, du Cadastre et de la Cartographie. This guarantees lawful ownership, cadastral verification, and protection against duplication or fraud.

DGI — Fiscal & Tax Compliance

All registrations, transfers, and property tax declarations are filed with Morocco’s Direction Générale des Impôts (DGI). We obtain tax clearance certificates and ensure every investment is fiscally compliant and transparent.

IFSA — International Financial Standards

Our international operations align with the standards of International Financial Services Authorities, ensuring anti-money-laundering (AML), know-your-customer (KYC), and transparent fund management procedures.

Licensing & Operational Authorization

Certified and licensed operations under Moroccan and international regulation.

  • Construction and land-use permits verified through local municipal authorities.
  • Registration and transfer oversight by national cadastral and tax departments.
  • All documents notarized and executed through certified Moroccan notarial offices.
  • Investor funds managed through verified escrow and regulated banking channels.
