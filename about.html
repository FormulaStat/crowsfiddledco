Our Origin & Expansion

From local roots to global reach.

Crowsfiddled began as a visionary idea to reshape how people invest in real estate — blending local market expertise with international best practices. Founded in Nigeria and now operating from Morocco, we serve as a bridge connecting Africa’s vibrant property markets with global investors and development partners.

We have an established presence across Lagos, Enugu, Asaba, Owerri, Umuahia, Abuja, and Jos, and continue to expand into strategic international cities that align with our mission of sustainable property growth.
